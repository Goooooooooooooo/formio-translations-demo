PhraseApp Instructions

If you are viewing this application via the our hosted service at apps.form.io/translations then the edit mode will be locked behind our Form.io PhraseApp account. To experience the integration yourself, please download this demo and replace the projectId and accessToken with your own inside the project environment folder.

If you are not sure how to do this, we've created a detailed walk-though that goes over creating the PhraseApp portion of the application. Feel free to antiquate yourself with PhraseApp interface as that particular section shows how to setup and retrieve a projectIdand accessToken.
