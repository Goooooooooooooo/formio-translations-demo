PhraseApp Instructions

If you are viewing this application via the our hosted service at apps.form.io/translations then the edit mode will be locked behind our Form.io PhraseApp account. To experience the integration yourself, please download this application here and replace the projectId and accessToken placeholders with your own to get started.

If you are not sure how to do this, we've created a detailed walk-though here that goes over creating just this portion of the application. Feel free to antiquate yourself with PhraseApp Interface specifically as that particular section shows how to setup and retrieve the tokens you'll need.
